Welcome to the Golf Simulator App

This app allows you to simulate a round of golf by customizing your club distances and playing through different holes. Simply enter the yardage for each club based on your potential maximum distance. Once set up, you can play through a round without having to look for yardage markers, making it easier and more enjoyable to focus on your game.

Instructions:
  1. Please enter the yardage for each club based on its potential maximum distance.
  2. Click 'Save' to Customized Club Distances.
  3. Next, click 'Start Round' button to start.
  4. Use the 'Swing' button to simulate your swing. The longer you hold the button, the more power you generate. After completing a hole, the next hole will load automatically.
